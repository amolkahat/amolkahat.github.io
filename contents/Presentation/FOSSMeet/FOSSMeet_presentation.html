What is Open Source?

  • Denoting software for which the original source code is made freely available and may be redistributed and modified. (Meaning)

  • The term "open source" refers to something people can modify and share because its design is publicly accessible. (Opensource.com)

  • Quality
  • Customization
  • Freedom
